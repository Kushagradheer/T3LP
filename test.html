Our Timber Specialties

We specialize in premium hardwood and softwood varieties from around the world

Hardwood Varieties

Hardwood comes from deciduous trees that lose their leaves annually. These slower growing trees produce denser wood with enhanced durability and strength.

White Ash

Known for its strength and elasticity, perfect for furniture and flooring.

White Oak

Highly durable with beautiful grain, ideal for cabinetry and barrels.

Walnut

Rich, dark wood prized for fine furniture and decorative veneers.

Poplar

Versatile and economical, great for painted furniture and millwork.

Red Oak

Strong and porous, excellent for flooring and interior trim.

Softwood Varieties

Softwood comes from coniferous trees that remain evergreen. These woods are easier to work with and more economical while still being versatile.

Southern Yellow Pine

Strong, stiff, and dense, perfect for construction and framing.

Other Softwoods

We supply various softwood species based on your project requirements.

Our Journey
